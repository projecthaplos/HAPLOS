The Automation Behind HAPLOS

A carefully orchestrated system transforms coconut husks into guava-infused pads with precision automation — ensuring consistency, efficiency, and bioactive reliability.

1. Cutting & Preparation

Coconut fibers are bleached, cleaned, and cut into uniform pad sizes using a precision hot-wire module. This ensures sealed edges and consistency.

2. First Infusion

Each fiber pad is evenly sprayed with guava leaf extract, followed by cassava starch binder to secure the infusion on the surface.

3. Flipping & Second Infusion

An automated pusher flips the pads for back-side treatment, ensuring both sides are infused with extract and binder for uniform bioactivity.

4. Controlled Drying

Pads are moved into a drying chamber with fan-driven airflow. Temperature and humidity sensors ensure stable drying without degrading antioxidants.

5. Quality Output

The finished gauze pads are uniform, bioactive, and cost-efficient — transforming agricultural waste into functional wound-care material.
